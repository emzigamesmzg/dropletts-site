How to play Dropletts

01

Letters fall

Tiles drop into a 5×5 grid, building up column by column. Act fast or the board fills up.

02

Tap to build words

Tap letters anywhere on the board to chain them into words. No swiping, just tapping.

03

Submit & clear

Valid words clear those tiles and score points. Longer words unlock powerful bonuses.

04

Beat the board

If a column hits the top it collapses. Lose all 5 columns and the game ends. Don't let it fill.

Skill-based bonuses

✨

5-letter word

Earn a Shiny tile — a ×2 score multiplier for your next word.

💣

6–7 letter word

Word Bomb! Clears a 3×3 chunk of the board when used.

🌟

8+ letter word

Full Grid Clear. The entire board wiped in one spectacular move.

➡️

Row / Column sweep

Use letters all in one line to wipe that full row or column clean.
